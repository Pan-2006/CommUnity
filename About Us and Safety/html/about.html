Who We Are

CommUnity is a student-centric peer-to-ride sharing application designed exclusively to resolve the daily transportation struggles of university students. By pairing student commuters with fellow peer drivers heading along the same university routes, we cultivate an interconnected network prioritizing convenience and accessibility.

Our framework reduces transit waiting lines, optimizes empty vehicle seats, minimizes greenhouse footprints across university sub-routes, and fosters collaborative community relationships straight from pickup barangays directly to campus gates.

Core System Values

Accessibility

Bridging transit gaps across remote routes directly to university campuses.

Affordability

Shared commute expenses customized explicitly for tight student budgets.

Accountability

Exclusive cross-verified institutional network matching riders cleanly.
